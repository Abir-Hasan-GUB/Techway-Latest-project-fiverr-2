About Techway Placements

We are helping raise the profile and change the career prospects of young people by giving them the opportunity to access quality and valuable tech work experience that is otherwise unavailable to them. Through our suite of programs, our expert team guides candidates in gaining placement and connecting with expert mentors, who are some of the best in the tech sector.

For the duration of four weeks, candidates will build work-relevant knowledge and aptitudes through a series of real-life, industry-level tasks and projects, while receiving personalized guidance, advice and support from their mentors. Through this extensive undertaking, they can have a positive taste of working life and gain solid experience that will enable them to secure and sustain employment in the tech sector.

Work experience reinvented

We know that despite being essential to their future career success, many young people do not always participate in work experience opportunities. Some of their constraints include economic and personal barriers, such as financial limitations and difficulty in accessing opportunities. To address these, our work experience programs are free to join and designed to be remotely accessible. Flexibility is also embedded into each program, allowing candidates to communicate with their mentors anytime and complete activities at their convenience.

Graduate vacancies have faced a downturn in recent years, and this is further compounded by the COVID-19 pandemic, making the immediate future of the job market uncertain.

Can't decide if you should pursue getting work experience? Let these facts and figures show you why it matters a lot as you make your way into the world of work.

  • It is recommended by the government

    • The UK government recommends that students should have at least 2 work experiences by the age of 18 in addition to any part-time job they hold to become more competitive.

  • It is an evidence of skills

    • An overwhelming majority of employers want to see work experience in CVs, as they deem it to be a validation of candidates' skills. The longer the placement, the greater the reward.

  • It is a valuable route to employment

    • Many employers invest more in work experience placements, seeing it as an excellent route to employment. 94% employers reportedly invite candidates who had a placement with them to return as employees.
